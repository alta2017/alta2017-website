Student Travel Support

ALTA will be offering travel support for students to attend and present at ALTA 2017 in Brisbane. The number of scholarships available, and their value, is still to be determined. These awards can be used to reimburse some of the travel and accommodation expenses. The organising committee reserves the right to make awards as they see fit, or to alter the amount of any awards.

Selected students for the awards will be contacted by the organising committee by mid November 2017. Preference will be given to students who will be presenting a paper at the workshop.
